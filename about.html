About Us

Rooted in tradition, expanding globally.

Our Roots in Telangana

Sri Laxmi Ganapathi Agritech was born out of a deep respect for the agricultural heritage of Telangana. What started as a local endeavor has blossomed into a trusted international export business.

We work directly with local farmers, ensuring that our sourcing process is ethical and that we are securing only the finest produce. By cutting out unnecessary middlemen, we guarantee that the premium Red Chillies and Maize we export are as fresh as the day they were harvested.

Our meticulous quality control—from sun-drying to mechanical sorting—ensures our clients worldwide receive products that consistently exceed international grading standards.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our every export.

Uncompromising Quality

We adhere to strict export grading systems, ensuring zero foreign matter, perfect moisture levels, and unparalleled freshness.

Farmer Partnership

We believe in growing together. By partnering directly with farmers, we ensure fair compensation and sustainable agriculture.

Global Reach

Our logistical expertise allows us to effortlessly ship high-volume metric tons across the globe reliably and safely.
